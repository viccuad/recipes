# Trulla's Happy Cookies (Angela's recipe)

30 pieces

* 2 eggs
* 120g of white sugar
* 120g of cane sugar
* 1 teaspoon of salt
* 1 teaspoon of soda
* 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
* 200g of soft butter
* 380g of flour
* 150g of chocolate

Chop chocolate to small pieces, make a dough from all ingredients. Add
chocolate.

Temperature: 150 ° C of heat from the top
Baking time: 25 minutes
